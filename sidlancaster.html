About the Author

Sid Lancaster crafts immersive fantasy and science fiction that transports readers to worlds where magic pulses through city streets, ancient powers awaken, and the powerless rise to challenge empires and gods.

Specializing in urban fantasy, epic fantasy, and supernatural tales with unique settings, Sid writes for young adult and adult audiences who crave stories that blur the line between the extraordinary and reality.

When not building new worlds, Sid is plotting the next chapter in the ongoing saga of servants, empires, and the twilight of suns.
